Hello everyone, in this post I would like to know about the first experience of using tinkoff-invest API. Used it to create a terminal application that rebalanced a portfolio.

Pros I found in the API: - fairly simple structure, easy to understand - Transparent distinction of access rights to accounts - Ability to experiment in the sandbox Minuses: - Sandbox is not stable
